Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Indiana Jones and the Lost Ark is the first movie in the Indiana Jones series. It came out in 1981 and was directed by Steven Spielberg and James Mangold. It is rated an 8.4/10 on IMDb.

Temple of Doom

Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom is the second movie in the Indiana Jones series. It came out in 1984 and had Harrison For playing Indiana Jones. It was only rated a 7.5/10 on IMDb.

The Last Crusade

Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade is the third movie in the Indiana Jones series. It cam out in 1989 and was the second highest grossing Indiana Jones movie, only second to the 2008 movie, Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull. It was rated an 8.2/10 on IMDb.
